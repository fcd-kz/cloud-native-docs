## Overview

Live Video Broadcasting (LVB) delivers professional cloud-based live streaming capabilities. With stable and scalable features such as live push, transcoding, distribution, and playback, LVB supports ultra-low latency, high image quality, and high performance even under very high concurrency.

> **Note**  
> You can use LVB after activating the Cloud Streaming Service (CSS). See *Getting Started* for setup guidance.

## Features

### Full Suite of Features
LVB supports RTMP push, HLS origin servers, and multiple live streaming sources. It provides a multi-platform live streaming SDK and supports adaptive bitrate streaming, beauty filters, audio processing, instant streaming, stutter reduction, screen recording, and seamless definition switching. Playback is supported across platforms, and custom player integration is also available.

---

### Global Acceleration
LVB leverages a large global CDN network to ensure low latency and reliable delivery across regions and network environments.

---

### Professional Video Processing
LVB offers cloud-based transcoding and media processing capable of handling large multimedia workloads. It supports format conversion for OTT services and playback on PCs and mobile devices, along with thumbnail generation, editing, content moderation, and encryption.

---

### Security and Hotlink Protection
LVB includes security mechanisms such as IP blocklists, referrer restrictions, HTTPS acceleration, URL encryption, and streaming encryption technologies. Digital rights management (DRM) solutions are also available.

---

### Intelligent Processing
AI-powered technologies enable automated image recognition, speech recognition, content moderation, subtitling, and other advanced media processing tasks.

---

### Statistics Analysis
Real-time monitoring of bandwidth, traffic, requests, and concurrent connections helps maintain stream quality and diagnose playback issues. Detailed statistics provide operational insights and support business scaling.
