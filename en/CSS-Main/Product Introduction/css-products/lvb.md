<h2>Features</h2>

<h3>Full suite of features</h3>
<p>
LVB supports RTMP push, HLS origin servers, and other live streaming sources. It provides a multi-platform live streaming SDK and supports adaptive bitrate streaming, beauty filters, audio processing, instant streaming, stutter reduction, screen recording, and seamless definition switching. Playback is supported on all platforms, and you can also customize your own player.
</p>

<h3>Global acceleration</h3>
<p>
CNS has over 2,000 CDN cache nodes across regions of the Chinese mainland, covering major ISPs. Outside the mainland, it operates more than 800 cache nodes in over 70 countries and regions across North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia.
</p>

<h3>Professional video processing</h3>
<p>
LVB provides cloud-based transcoding and processing services that can handle vast multimedia data. It can transcode audio and video files into formats suitable for OTT services or playback on PC and mobile devices. Features include thumbnail generation, editing, content moderation, and encryption.
</p>

<h3>Security safeguarding and hotlink protection</h3>
<p>
LVB provides all-round protection to prevent unauthorized playback of your videos, including IP blocklists, referrer blocklists, secure HTTPS acceleration, URL encryption, and live streaming encryption technologies. It also offers a professional DRM solution to safeguard your video assets.
</p>

<h3>Intelligent learning</h3>
<p>
With powerful AI-based recognition technology, LVB can learn and analyze video or image content. This enables intelligent automation of image recognition, speech recognition, content moderation, subtitling, and other tasks.
</p>

<h3>Statistics analysis</h3>
<p>
Based on big data analysis covering different industry scenarios, LVB enables real-time control of push quality and identification of playback issues. It monitors metrics such as bandwidth, traffic, requests, and concurrent connections in real time and provides detailed statistical results to help scale your business.
</p>
